About Us

The collective term 'Carmelite Family' refers to the different types of people who feel inspired by the spirituality of Carmel.

The Camelite Family includes:

  • Fliars
  • Hermits
  • Enclosed nuns
  • Active nuns
  • Active sisters
  • Lay people

All these people live out the Carmelite way of life quite differently according to their own circumstances and in juridically distinct institutions
yet they are united as one great family through the sharing of Carmel's spiritual heritage.
